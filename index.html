About Section

I love video games, I'm super interested in all things design, I play the guitar, I love all things tech, and my creativity is my strongest motivator. All that being said HTML really stood out to me as a good programming language to learn because of its simplicity and ease of use for a beginner programmer like myself. I have so many ideas swimming up in this head of mine and really have no clue how to get them out and onto a "canvas", but I wanted to learn how to take some of the ideas I had and get them out there not just in writing but in creation. My love for video games plus the creative part of me instantly thought about going the game dev route, So I decided to switch things up and work my way up to game making, I joined this Bootcamp called Generation USA learning to be a Junior Web Developer and this is my story begins.
